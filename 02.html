Context

- wikipedia
- constantly edited
- usually only latest version accessed
- revision history a reflection of how public knowledge and discussion around HIV/AIDS changes over time

Data Acquisition

- data is derived from more than 8'000 versions of the HIV/AIDS wikipedia article
- filtered around three chosen keywords: condoms, viral load, safe sex
- paragraphs that contain the keyword are extracted

Usage

- the paragraphs are positioned vertically according the time they have been written
- move the mouse left and right to select a paragraph
- scroll up and down to see how a particular paragraph has been changing over time
